Beware of Fraudulent people posing as Recruiters!

It has come to our notice that certain unscrupulous people are posing as placement agents and are promising job seekers with opportunities at Reliance Retail, in return for money. Reliance Retail is a professional and responsible corporate citizen of India and will never charge any money from job seekers, in exchange for jobs. Job seekers are therefore advised to be cautious of fraudulent placement agencies and individuals.

Also, please note that Reliance Retail will not be liable for the consequences of any transactions between job seekers and such fraudulent placement agencies and individuals.
